What We Do

Engineering services built for real-world manufacture

01

3D CAD Modeling

Parametric part and assembly modeling in SolidWorks and Fusion 360, built to your tolerances and ready for downstream manufacture or simulation.

02

Industrial Design

Aesthetic direction, ergonomics, and form development — translating briefs and sketches into cohesive product language that resonates with users.

03

DFM / DFA

Design for Manufacture and Assembly review — identifying cost drivers, tooling constraints, and process compatibility before you cut steel or place tooling orders.

04

Technical Documentation

Engineering drawings, BOM generation, and GD&T annotation prepared to ISO or ASME standards, ready for suppliers and manufacturing partners.

05

FEA Simulation

Structural and thermal finite element analysis to validate designs under real-world loading before physical prototypes, saving time and reducing redesign risk.

06

Prototype Support

File preparation for 3D printing, CNC machining, or injection mould trials — including tolerance stackup review and first-article support documentation.

About the Studio

Where craft meets engineering discipline

The Aura Nest is a specialist design studio founded on a single conviction: that beautiful products and manufacturable products are not in tension — they are the same product, resolved with enough care.

With a BE in Mechanical Engineering and an MS in Industrial Design, the studio bridges the gap between aesthetic intent and production reality across 300+ delivered projects for clients in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and Germany.

Every engagement is treated as a collaborative engineering problem — not a service ticket. From first concept to final drawing package, clients receive rigorous technical output paired with a considered design sensibility.

How We Work

A structured path from brief to build

01

Discovery Call

We understand your product goals, manufacturing context, constraints, and timeline. No generic intake forms — a real technical conversation.
